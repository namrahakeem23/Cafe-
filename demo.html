ABOUT
The Penthouse Café in Aliganj, Lucknow, is a vibrant rooftop dining destination known for its eclectic menu and lively ambiance. It offers a diverse range of cuisines, including North Indian, Chinese, Continental, and BBQ, catering to a variety of palates. The café is particularly popular for its rooftop setting, providing patrons with a relaxed atmosphere complemented by music and hookah options. With an average cost of ₹950 for two people, it serves as an ideal spot for casual outings and gatherings.
FOUNDER
The Penthouse Café was founded by Lucknow-based entrepreneur Luckhmeet Singh Saluja, popularly known as Raj Saluja. A graduate of Lucknow University with a specialization in business administration and management, Raj ventured into the café and lounge business, establishing The Penthouse Café in Aliganj. Beyond the café, he has been involved in organizing major events in Lucknow, such as the Holi festival "Color Jam – The Holi Fest," featuring numerous artists and entertainers. Raj emphasizes the importance of quality and customer loyalty in the competitive restaurant industry and plans to expand further into the food and restaurant sector in the coming years
